Our Facilities

The infrastructure facilities offered by the college.

World Class Library

Each department has its own library.
The central library currently has 163152 volumes of books of which 5121 books belong to the rare collection category.
It also provides access to around 209 print journals.

Largest Play Ground

Our College provides spacious playfields for Football, Volleyball, Basket ball, Kabaddi, Handball and Ball Badminton. Our students’ most favourite game is football and they show immense interest in it.

Two Types Of Hostels

St Joseph's College facilities include state of the art hostel for both girls and boys.
The hostel will be provided only after the students gets admission into any of the courses provided by the college and after an interview with the Director.
The parent needs to be present at the time of admission.
